Mama Betty's Pasta Sauce

History:

This sauce recipe has been in my family for over 100 years. If you make the recipe I hope you enjoy it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large yellow onion - chopped
  • 28 oz can of Roma or San Marino Tomatos
  • 3 large carrots - peeled into ribbons
  • 1 1/2 cup Sweet Red Wine
  • Basil
  • 3 or 4 bay leaves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Spicy Italian Sausage (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, heat olive oil until shimmering.
  2. Reduce heat to medium low and add onions, bay leaves and a generous amount of basil
  3. When onions are translucent add carrot ribbons and half of a cup of wine. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and the rest of the wine, if using sausage add as well - simmer for 45 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  5. Remove bay leaves and blend 3/4 of the sauce.
  6. Enjoy with pasta, lasagna, or over eggplant.
